I have research experience across Yale University and University College London in computational psychiatry, affective neuroscience, and clinical psychology. My work spans computational modelling, ecological momentary assessment (EMA), longitudinal analysis, and neuroimaging methods applied to mood disorders and addiction.

I have contributed to multiple international collaborative projects, conference presentations, and ongoing publications. I am broadly interested in developing mechanistic models of emotion and psychopathology with translational clinical relevance.
